polar

(method of tensor)

compute the polar decomposition of rank 2 tensors

Syntax 

[R,SR] = polar(T)
[R,SR,SL] = polar(T)

Input

T

rank 2 tensor

Output

R

orthogonal rank 2 tensor
SR

symmetric rank 2 tensor such that R * SR = T
SL

symmetric rank 2 tensor such that SL * R = T
